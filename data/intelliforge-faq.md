## About IntelliForge

IntelliForge is an AI engineering bootcamp founded by Girish Hiremath. The mission
is to take Python developers from "I have heard of LLMs" to "I ship deployed AI
systems that earn money" in 12 weeks. It is hands-on, project-based, and
explicitly anti-theory: every week ends with a deployed artifact.

Girish has 14+ years of experience building software for Fortune 500 companies
across six verticals, including payments, healthcare, manufacturing, and
education technology. The full track record is at https://gaas.intelliforge.tech/.

## Bootcamp duration and format

- **Duration:** 12 weeks (84 calendar days)

## Who the bootcamp is for

The bootcamp is built for:

- Python developers with 1 to 5 years of experience who have never shipped an
  LLM-powered application
- Final-year MTech and BTech students entering the 2026 job market
- Working backend or data engineers evaluating a pivot into AI engineering
- Aspiring solo founders who want to test the "micro-SaaS in a weekend" idea

The bootcamp is **not** for:

- Absolute Python beginners (we recommend a beginner Python course first)
- People expecting a placement guarantee (we help with placement; we don't
  promise a specific company or salary)
- Pure prompt engineers who don't want to write production code

## Curriculum overview (high level)

| Week | Theme |
|------|-------|
| 1-2 | LLM foundations, prompt engineering, function calling |
| 3-4 | Tool-using agents, ReAct loop, memory, evaluation |
| 5-6 | Multi-agent systems and orchestration patterns |
| 7-8 | Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), embeddings, vector DBs |
| 9-10 | Production deployment - FastAPI, Docker, Fly.io, observability |
| 11-12 | Capstone - ship a monetizable micro-SaaS or freelance deliverable |

Every week includes a live coding session, a graded project, and a 1:1 review.

## Technology stack used in the bootcamp

- **Language:** Python 3.11+ (with `uv` for environment management)
- **LLM providers:** OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq, plus local models via Ollama
- **Frameworks:** Direct SDK + FastAPI for production. We introduce LangChain,
  LangGraph, and the OpenAI Agents SDK in Week 5+ once students understand
  the primitives.
- **Vector DBs:** ChromaDB for development, Pinecone or pgvector for production
- **Deployment targets:** Fly.io and Vercel (depending on app shape)
- **AI-assisted IDE:** Cursor (used heavily in Week 11-12 for vibe coding)

## Common questions

**Q: Will I get a job offer at the end?**
A: We do not guarantee specific offers. We do guarantee placement *support* -
mock interviews, resume reviews, intros to hiring partners. Roughly 70% of
graduates land an AI-relevant role within 90 days of finishing the program.

**Q: Can I do this while working a full-time job?**
A: Yes. Most students do. The 10-15 hour weekly commitment is designed to fit
into evenings and weekends. The live sessions are on Tue/Thu evenings IST.

**Q: Do I need a beefy laptop or a GPU?**
A: No. Everything we ship runs against hosted LLM APIs. A laptop with 8 GB RAM
that can run Python and Docker is sufficient.

**Q: What if I miss a live session?**
A: Every session is recorded and posted within 24 hours. We do recommend
attending live for at least the first 4 weeks - that is when the cohort
relationships form.

**Q: Is there a payment plan?**
A: Yes. 3-month and 6-month no-cost EMI options are available at checkout.

**Q: Can my employer pay for the bootcamp?**
A: Yes. We provide GST-compliant invoices and corporate purchase orders on
request. Email hello@intelliforge.tech with your company details.
