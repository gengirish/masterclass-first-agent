## Curriculum — 12 weeks, three phases

Every week ends with something **deployed** — not something studied.

### Phase 1: Ship Your First Agent (Weeks 1–4)

**Phase outcome:** Deploy a working AI agent with tool calling to a live endpoint.

| Week | Theme | You ship |
|------|-------|----------|
| 1 | LLM Fundamentals & Prompt Engineering | Structured-output API you can demo in an interview |
| 2 | RAG Pipelines & Vector Databases | Document Q&A system with vector search — deployed |
| 3 | Tool Calling & Single Agent Systems | Single agent with tools, memory, and error handling |
| 4 | LangGraph & Agent Workflows | Graph-based agent workflow with human-in-the-loop |

Week 1 topics: LLM architecture & tokenization, advanced prompt engineering,
OpenAI/Anthropic/open-source APIs, structured outputs & function calling.

Week 2 topics: Embedding models & vector stores (Pinecone, ChromaDB), chunking &
retrieval optimization, production RAG with LangChain, evaluation & quality metrics.

Week 3 topics: Function/tool calling patterns, building your first AI agent, agent
memory & state management, error handling & fallback strategies.

Week 4 topics: Graph-based agent orchestration, state machines for AI workflows,
human-in-the-loop patterns, conditional branching & routing.

### Phase 2: Ship Multi-Agent Systems (Weeks 5–8)

**Phase outcome:** Ship a multi-agent system with enterprise integration patterns.

| Week | Theme | You ship |
|------|-------|----------|
| 5 | Multi-Agent Architecture | Crew of specialized agents collaborating on a task |
| 6 | Enterprise Integration Patterns | Agent integrated with real business APIs and RBAC |
| 7 | Production Deployment & DevOps | Containerized agent on CI/CD with monitoring |
| 8 | AI Workflow Automation | Webhook-driven automation connecting agents to Slack/email |

Week 5 topics: Role specialization & hierarchical agents, CrewAI & multi-agent
frameworks, inter-agent communication, conflict resolution & consensus.

Week 6 topics: Banking & Fintech AI use cases, Healthcare & Pharma compliance,
API orchestration & middleware, security, audit trails & RBAC.

Week 7 topics: Docker containerization for AI agents, CI/CD (GitHub Actions),
AWS Bedrock & cloud deployment, AgentOps, monitoring & observability.

Week 8 topics: n8n & automation platforms, webhook-driven agent triggers, email/Slack/CRM
integrations, building automation as a service.

### Phase 3: Ship Your Product (Weeks 9–12)

**Phase outcome:** Launch an AI product — or contribute to a live IntelliForge repo.

| Week | Theme | You ship |
|------|-------|----------|
| 9 | Vibe Coding — AI-Assisted Development | Full-stack app scaffolded and deployed with AI assistants |
| 10 | Building Your AI Micro-SaaS | Landing page, auth, and payment integration live |
| 11 | Launch & Growth | Product live with analytics and support automation |
| 12 | Demo Day & Credential Issuance | Verifiable credential with your shipped-product link |

Week 9 topics: Cursor, Windsurf & AI coding assistants, prompt-to-product workflows,
full-stack scaffolding with AI, code review & quality with AI.

Week 10 topics: Idea validation & market research with AI, landing page & payment
integration, user auth/dashboards/admin panels, pricing & monetization models.

Week 11 topics: Product launch playbook, SEO & content marketing with AI, customer
support automation, analytics & iteration.

Week 12 topics: Product demo & shipped-product review, mentor scoring session,
credential issuance via certs.intelliforge.tech, share your win on share.intelliforge.tech.
